Introduction to Electromagnetic Waves

In physics, mathematics, engineering, and related fields, a wave is a propagating dynamic disturbance (change from equilibrium) of one or more quantities.

There are two types of waves that are most commonly studied in classical physics: mechanical waves and electromagnetic waves

Electromagnetic waves are emitted by electrically charged particles undergoing acceleration.
